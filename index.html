Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress through cutting-edge technology, research, and creativity, fostering a culture of entrepreneurship and problem-solving to create transformative solutions for a competitive and thriving economy.

Sustainability

Sustainability prioritizes eco-friendly practices, renewable energy, and conservation, ensuring balanced development that preserves natural resources, mitigates climate change, and secures a healthy environment for future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure focuses on modernizing transportation, energy, and technology systems, building resilient networks, and enhancing connectivity to drive economic growth, urban development, and improved quality of life.

Empowerment

Empowerment emphasizes uplifting individuals through education, skill development, and equal opportunities, fostering self-reliance, innovation, and inclusive growth for a resilient and prosperous nation.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
